Detailed Description

Specializes the MultiSpanSliderTracker s.t. it also displays the color assigned to the tracker within a ColorMapEditor.

The painter needs to be associated with a ColorMapEditor by the invocation ColorMapSpanPainter::setWidget of to work properly.
